Leopold Bloom Art Award

The Leopold Bloom Art Award is aimed at supporting progressive contemporary visual artists in Hungary and their presence in the international art scene. The award was established in 2011 and is now a bi-annual event.

It was set up by John Ward & MAry Mc Loughlin of the Irish Maurice Ward Group, in co-operation with the Ludwig Museum, ACAX/Agency for Contemporary Art Exchange.
