Art Box

At Maurice Ward Art Handling our principal interest is the safe and secure transport of your artworks. We are now offering an Art Box product that is designed for sending relatively small, flat, inexpensive artworks from your home, gallery or office to any destination worldwide without the need to avail of a dedicated fine art service.

Click here to download instructions.

What is Art Box?
Art Box is a safe, insured, affordable international art shipping service, door to door.

Constructed from sturdy, thick corrugated cardboard, the Art Box has a polyethylene foam interior lining and polythene wrap.

Our experience moving rare and unique works, coupled with our wide reach means that your precious objects are surrounded by 360 degrees of care.
